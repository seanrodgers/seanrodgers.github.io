Predicting the champion of any sport is a near impossible task. It always seems that rocket science would be a better challenge. As the title said, this is the prediction for the 2025 Stanley Cup winner. A look at regular season rank, goals for and against per game, powerplay, penalty kill and save percentage are the key statistics looked at to find Lord Stanley’s next home.

A hockey team’s rank can tell a lot about their playoff performance. In the NHL, the last team who won the President’s Trophy that also won the Stanley Cup was the Chicago Blackhawks in 2013. Since then, there has not been a team to accomplish the two hardest trophies to win in sports.

In the past 10 seasons in the NHL, excluding the shortened seasons for the global Covid19 pandemic, no such team did what Chicago did. The closest teams to do this were the 2022 Colorado Avalanche and the 2017 Pittsburgh Penguins. This knocks the Winnipeg Jets off the list of teams to win the Stanley Cup.

The main object in hockey, as well as many other sports, is to score more than the opponent. Goals. Something every hockey player remembers no matter the age. There were many teams that were close in the goals for per game as well as the goals against stats, but one team stood above the rest.

The Tampa Bay Lightning.

Code 
playoffgoals <- playoffs25 |>

  labs(

Of course, they were knocked off by the Florida Panthers early in round one, so this eliminates the Lightning.

Keeping along the same theme with goals for and against, powerplay chances and penalty kill efficiency play a monumental role in winning hockey games.

Code

Once again, the key to winning hockey games is scoring more than your opponent. To do this, goaltenders come into play. Winnipeg comes in at the top in save percentage by a considerable amount with the Dallas Stars nipping at their heels.

Now for the moment that everyone has been waiting for.

The 2025 Stanley Cup prediction winner is the…

Vegas Golden Knights.
